What You'll Learn

Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, my lessons cover a variety of techniques, music theory, and genres. Here's an overview of what you'll learn:

Guitar Techniques

Technique is the foundation of becoming a great guitarist. In my lessons, you will learn:

  • Proper hand posture and positioning
  • How to hold a pick
  • Holding the guitar neck
  • Basic to advanced strumming patterns
  • Fingerpicking techniques
  • Chords: open, barre, power chords, and more
  • Scale patterns and soloing techniques
  • Advanced techniques such as bends, slides, and vibrato

Basic Music Theory

Understanding music theory can greatly improve your playing. You’ll get a solid foundation in:

  • Note reading (Sheet Music and tabs)
  • Intervals, scales, and modes
  • Chord construction (major, minor)
  • Key signatures and their relation to chords
  • Feeling rhythm and adapting to a band or director
  • Building and recognizing chord progressions

Genres I Teach

I can teach you songs and techniques from a wide range of genres. These include:

  • Classic Rock: Led Zeppelin, ACDC, The Eagles, and more
  • Worship: Learning contemporary Christian music and church hymns
  • Jazz: Chord voicings, improvisation, and jazz chord progressions
  • Big Band: An extension of jazz, learning swing rhythms and strong chord voicing
  • Metal: Heavy riffs, palm muting, fast picking techniques
  • Pop: Modern pop songs and understanding how to play melodies
  • Country: The old classics to the new stuff, Tim McGraw, Johnny Cash, to now
  • Rock 'n' Roll: From the 50s to the present day, Elvis, Jimi Hendrix, etc.

If you're interested in learning any of these areas or want to develop your skills further, feel free to reach out and book a lesson!
